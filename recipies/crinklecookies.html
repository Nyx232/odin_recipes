Lemon Crinkle Cookies

Description

These Lemon Crinkle Cookies are soft and moist and one of my all time favorite spring and summer desserts. They are bursting with flavor and never disappoint at a good picnic! They are super easy to make and are SO tasty!

The cookies have a soft and gooy inside but the powdered sugar coats the slight shell to the cookie perfectly making these cookies extra special and quite gorgeous too!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter, Softened
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon lemon extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. In the bowl of a stand mixer, cream butter and sugar together until light and fluffy. Mix in vanilla, lemon extract, egg, lemon zest, and lemon juice. Scrape sides and mix again.
  3. In a medium sized mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
  4. With standmixer on medium-low speed, gradually add the flour mixture. Beat until just combined.
  5. Refrigerate dough for at least one hour, or overnight.
  6. Pour powdered sugar onto a bowl/plate.
  7. Roll a heaping tablespoon of dough into a ball then roll in powdered sugar until well coated.
  8. Place on lined baking sheet and repeat with remaining dough, keeping the alls of dough two inches apart on baking sheet.
  9. Bake for 9-11 Minutes or until cookies are cracked on top.
  10. Remove from oven and let cookies sit for about three minutes before transferring to a cooling rack. ENJOY!
