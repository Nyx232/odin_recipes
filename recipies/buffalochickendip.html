Buffalo Chicken Dip

Description

This simple but delicious Buffalo Chicken Dip recipe is the perfect game day recipe or party appetizer! Filled with chicken, hot sauce, ranch, and a variety of cheeses, then baked until bubbly, this is a crowd-pleasing dip that comes together quickly. Creamy, cheesy, and mouthwatering, this is the ultimate dip.

Made with simple ingredients, this buffalo chicken dip is a creamy and ultra-cheesy dip that is perfect for any occasion. It makes for the ultimate party food as it is easy to prepare, serve, and eat as well. It's great for sharing and always disappears first.

Ingredients

  • 2(8ounce/277g) blocks cream cheese softened.
  • 3/4 cup Frank's Original Red Hot Sauce (180ml)
  • 1/2 cup ranch dressing (120ml)
  • 8 ounces shredded cheddar cheese divided
  • 8 ounces crumbled blue cheese
  • 3 cups cooked shredded chicken about 1 rotisserie chicken
  • Celery sticks and tortilla chips to serve

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cream cheese, Frank's hot sauce, and ranch. Stir together until smooth. (You can use an electric hand or stand mixer if desired.)
  3. Stir in the 6 ounces of cheddar, blue cheese, and green onion. Stir in the shredded chicken until well incorporated. Transfer the mixture to an 8Ã—8-inch baking dish and sprinkle with the remaining cheddar cheese.
  4. Bake until mixture is bubbling, about 25 to 30 minutes.
  5. Let dip cool for at least 15 minutes before serving. Sprinkle with remaining green onion just before serving. Serve with celery sticks and tortilla chips.
